Programming for Kids

At Maya Kids Digital School, we believe that learning programming should be fun and engaging. Our courses are designed to introduce children to the world of coding through interactive projects and games.

  • Python Basics: Dive into the fundamentals of Python. Price: $120

  • App Creation: Make mobile applications. Price: $160
  • Digital Art & Animation: Create animated stories. Price: $140

Why Choose Our Programs?

Our experienced instructors provide personalized attention, ensuring that each child progresses at their own pace while having fun. We focus on creativity, problem-solving, and teamwork, making coding an enjoyable experience for the whole family!
